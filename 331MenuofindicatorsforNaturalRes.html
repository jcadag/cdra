3.3.1 Menu of indicators for natural resource based production areas exposure units


The list of indicators for Natural Resource-based Production Areas exposure database is provided in the table below. For hazard and exposure columns, the indicators need not be changed unless there are other indicators that are more suitable. For sensitivities and adaptive capacities, the choice of indicators suggested below depend on the available data. But the suggested indicators are:


Sensitivities:


    • Total Number of Dependent HHs or Families to affected areas
    • Affected areas without irrigation facilities (for agricultural croplands and ricelands)
    • Affected areas without water impounding (for agricultural croplands and ricelands)
    • Owners/areas not employing sustainable production techniques


Adaptive capacities:


    • Dependent HHs with access to alternative livelihood
    • Affected areas covered or protected by hazard mitigation measures/structures (e.g. slope protection, seawall, flood control, etc.)
    • Production areas covered by post-disaster economic protection (e.g. insurance. micro-finance)
    • Insurance Coverage (Est. percentage of property covered by insurance)(early warning system,  local awareness on climate change and natural hazards)
    • Government Investments (Capacity of government to make investments in CCA-DRR (Institutional, financial and technical capacity to provide local agriculture and forestry-based extension programs  such as technology and knowledge transfer )
