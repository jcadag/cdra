5.3  Disaster Risk Assessment Mapping (DRA Mapping)


DRA mapping translates the DRA table to a more visual illustration i.e. risk maps. From the risk tables indicating risk for each barangay (or any sections of the territory), colors indicating the risk level are assigned.
