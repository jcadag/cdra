1.1.1 Seasonal temperature and seasonal rainfall changes


Instructions:


    • Fill in Table below to summarize data on climate change projection. PAGASA already provides data per province on its most recent publication of Observed and Projected Climate Change in the Philippines (2018).
    • Determine projected seasonal temperature change and projected seasonal rainfall change in 2050 (2036-2065) and 2085 (2070-2099) based on the observed baseline data.
    • For seasonal temperature, add changes in 2050 to the observed baseline data. Example: 26.9 (Observed baseline for DJF) + 1.2 (Change in 2050 for DJF) = 28.1 degree Celsius.
    • For seasonal rainfall, add changes in 2050 to the observed baseline data. Example: 376 (Observed baseline for DJF) + 67.5% (Change in 2050 for DJF) = 629.8 millimetre.
