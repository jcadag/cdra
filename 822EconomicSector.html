8.2.2 Economic Sector

8.2.2 Economic Sector


Economic sector refers to land uses for production purposes. Based on the results of CDRA (e.g. commercial areas considered as high risk), adjustments in area requirements for urban uses and natural-based resource production areas may be needed. For urban uses, situational analysis refers to computation of adjusted land requirements based on projected land requirement and results of risk and vulnerability analysis (e.g. additional land to accommodate land uses that need to be relocated due to high risk status).
