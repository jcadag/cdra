    • Adjustments in housing requirements
    • Area requirements for educational facilities
    • Adjustments in area requirements for Health related facilities

Area requirements for educational facilities


Instructions:


    • Compute for area requirements for educational facilities (fill in Table 8.2.1.2)
    • Identify school/ educational facilities per barangay including their attributes such as:
      • Projected classroom requirements
      • Existing classroom
      • Gap
    • Compute for the number of classrooms needed based on the results of CDRA
      • Classrooms for relocation
      • Available classrooms after CDRA
    • Compute for area requirements (in hectare) to provide sufficient space for needed educational facilities

Adjustments in area requirements for health related facilities


Instructions:


    • Compute for area requirements for health facilities (fill in Table 8.2.1.3)
    • Identify health facilities per barangay including related attributes such as:
      • Projected population per barangay
      • Projected BHS requirements
      • Current facilities
    • Compute for the number of health facilities needed based on the results of CDRA
      • Health related facilities for relocation
      • Available health related facilities after CDRA
    • Compute for area requirements (in hectare) to provide sufficient space for needed health facilities
