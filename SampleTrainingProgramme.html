PM

HOMEWARD BOUND


    • NOTES TO TRAINERS:
      • Inform LGUs to have a mapper present in the activity. Laptops should have GIS (preferably ArcGIS or QGIS)
      • Inform LGUs to bring their own data (e.g. shapefiles, CBMS, Sectoral Studies, hazard maps) and preliminary CDRA outputs, if available
      • Sample datasets will be used, unless the LGU has its own data
      • The process will be more on completing the Excel Tables required for CDRA based on the Supplemental Guidelines
      • Climate Change Vulnerability Assessment was tweaked using the AR5 framework.
      • Conduct recap and synthesis sessions every day.
      • Have LGUs present their outputs.
      • Make printouts of tables and matrices, as well as the scoring parameters.
      • Color tables fields used for estimating risks and vulnerabilities.
      • Prepare raffle bowl with the names of the LGUs for different sessions of the training.
      • CVR will support PDG in the conduct the training sessions/workshops.
    • THINGS FOR CVR TO BRING:
      • Metacards
      • Manila Paper
      • Pentel Pen
      • Tape
      • Extension wires
      • Printer and papers
      • Clicker
      • Notebooks and ballpens
    • TAKE HOME ASSIGNMENTS/ EXPECTED OUTPUTS AS INPUTS TO NEXT MODULE:
      • Accomplished tabulated data/information
      • Discussion on climate projection and analysis
      • Climate Impact Chain per relevant sector/Climate stimuli
      • Hazard Maps
      • Exposure Maps (Population, Critical Facilities, Lifeline Facilities, Urban Area and Natural Resource))
      • Revised sectoral studies integrating technical findings and mainstreaming CCA and DRR
