Climate variables

General Changes Expected in Climate Variables

Temperature

Increasing in temperature for all seasons expected in 2020 and 2050

Rainfall

Increasing average rainfall rate for all seasons except in MAM and DJA in 2020 and MAM in 2050

Number of Hot days

Slight decrease in 2020 to be followed by a great increase in 2050 (from 29 days in 1971-2000 to 297 days in 2050)

Number of Dry days

Reduction of at least 1,981 and 1979 dry days for 2020 and 2050, respectively

Extreme daily Rainfall Events

Additional 5 days of extreme rainfall events (from 2 to 7 days for 2020 and 2050 respectively)

Sea Level

A minimum 0.26 m and maximum of 0.85 sea level rise are to be expected
