6.2 Identification and Mapping of Major Decision Areas (MDAs)

6.1 Identification and Mapping of Major Decision Areas (MDAs)


Instructions:


    • Identify and map Major Decision Areas (MDAs). MDAs are high risk areas where communities are most exposed to hazards and/or where population and other exposure units (i.e. urban uses, natural resource production areas, critical facilities and lifeline utilities) are more vulnerable.
      • Based on the results of risk assessment (Step 4 and Step 5), produce a map that shows or highlights the MDAs or the location of the areas with higher (or highest) risks.
      • Identify MDAs (i.e. MDA 1 - 4). The number of MDA depends on the decision of the LGU or users.
