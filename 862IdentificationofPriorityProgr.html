8.6.2 Identification of Priority Programs and Projects


This steps identifies the priority programs, projects and activities (PPAs) in support of the realization of the CLUP. Supposedly, following the development thrusts and spatial strategies developed in the previous steps, the PPAs in this step should also be risk-sensitive.


Instructions:


    • Fill in Template 8.6.2.
    • Identify set of goals, objectives, and success indicators; and the operationalization of the desired land use scheme and policies (see sample below).


Template 8.6.2: Priority Programs-Projects-Legislation (sample)


Vision Statement

Goals

Objectives

Programs, Projects, Activities, and Legislation

 A vibrant and sustainable commercial, industrial, and tourism-based economy propelled by proactive and self-reliant citizenry living in a water sufficient, adaptive, and balanced environment with competitive and pro-poor governance.

 

Ensure the adequate and efficient delivery of basic social support facilities/ services
  • Establishment of 22 new BHS that are disaster and climate risk resilient by 2023
  • Establishment of additional 139 classrooms that are disaster and climate risk resilient in safe locations and reduce cases of disruption of classes due to structural and equipment damage
  • Opol Zero backlog health program • Hazard retro tting of existing health related facilities
  • Opol education modernization program • Hazard retrofitting of existing educational related facilities

 Ensure human security by increasing the level of adaptive capacities of the population and establishment of sustainable and well adapted units
  • Increase level of awareness of local population on emerging issues related to disasters and climate change including measures for adaptation and mitigation
  • Encourage the retro tting/upgrading of 405 existing housing structures
  • Formulation of the municipal and barangay level DRRM Plans • Formulation of the Local Climate Change Action Plan • Formulation of contingency plans for various hazards • Storm Surge Modeling Project • Earthquake Hazard Modeling and Risk Assessment • Establishment of the Building Information Management System (BIMS)

Refer to HLURB, 2015 (p. 250-252) for more examples
