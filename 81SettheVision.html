8.1 Set the Vision

       Entry point to 12-Step CLUP Process:


Setting the vision is Step 3 of the 12-Step CLUP process. After risk and vulnerability assessment (i.e. 6 Step CDRA), the mainstreaming of key findings and results in CLUP starts by influencing the vision. HLURB emphasizes that "integration of the results of the CDRA intends to guide the formulation of a risk-sensitive comprehensive land use plan towards a safer and resilient human settlements through rationalized location of people, assets, economic activities, and sustainable management of resources to effectively manage risks and address the challenges posed by hazards and climate change" (HLURB, 2014: p. 197).
