8.4 Selection of the Preferred Development Thrust

       Entry point to 12-Step CLUP Process:


The development thrust is the priority and long term development objectives of the LGUs. While all LGUs may strive to become commercial or industrial centers of their province or region, they should recognize that these development objectives should depend on their inherent potentials and opportunities in terms of resources, location, historical and current role, etc. LGUs are then encouraged to identify at least three development thrust options (i.e. Agriculture, Industrial, Commercial, Eco-Tourism, Agri-Tourism) and prepare an evaluation criteria to select the preferred thrust based on several criteria.


Instructions:


    • Conduct evaluation of development thrusts (Table 8.4.1)
    • Identify at least three development thrust options (Alternative 1-3)
    • List down criteria for selection (e.g. Cost, consistency with vision, etc.)
    • Provide weight for each criteria (in this example, 50%-50% for 2 criteria)
    • Provide a rating (1-4) for each alternative using the following scoring guide:
      • 1 - Low contribution to the achievement of desired goals, very high cost requirements to achieve goals, very limited capacities and/or capabilities of constituents or the government to achieve goals
      • 2 - Moderate contribution to the achievement of desired goals, high cost requirements to achieve goals, limited capacities and/or capabilities of constituents or the government to achieve goals
      • 3 - High contribution to the achievement of desired goals, moderate cost requirements to achieve goals, existing capacities and/or capabilities of constituents or the government to achieve goals are present
      • 4 - Very high contribution to the achievement of desired goals, Low cost requirements to achieve goals, existing capacities and/or capabilities of constituents or the government to achieve goals are highly compatible
    • Compute for the weighted rating
    • Compute for the total rating
    • Rank the preferred development thrusts according to the result
