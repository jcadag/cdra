3.1.1 Menu of indicators for population exposure units

3.1.1 Menu of indicators for population exposure unit


The list of indicators for population exposure database is provided in the table below. For hazard and exposure columns, the indicators need not be changed unless there are other indicators that are more suitable. For sensitivities and adaptive capacities, the choice of indicators suggested below depend on the available data. But the suggested indicators are:


Sensitivities:


    • Informal Settlers (IS)
    • People living in dwelling units made from light materials or salvageable materials
    • Young dependents (<= 5 years old)
    • Old dependents (>= 65 years old)
    • Persons with disabilities (mental and physical)/ chronic diseases
    • Families below the poverty threshold


Adaptive capacities:


    • People with access to infrastructure-related mitigation measures
    • People with access to financial assistance
    • People with capacity and willingness to retrofit or relocate
    • People with access to information
    • People who benefit from government investments on CCA-DRR
